// What's NOT in the price

What's NOT in the price.

Zero transaction fees

We don't take a percentage of your volume. Your revenue is your revenue. Our fee is fixed, regardless of your scale.

Zero gas costs

Our settlement layer abstracts blockchain network fees. Agents pay micro-amounts (~$0.001/tx) — you pay the subscription, nothing more.

Zero compliance overhead

No PSP fees, no chargebacks, no hold periods. We never custody funds, so you never inherit our compliance.

// Protocol FAQ

Protocol FAQ.

Do you take a percentage of agent payments? expand_more

No. Agents pay you directly in USDC. We charge a flat monthly SaaS fee. Period.

Do you hold or custody our funds? expand_more

Never. USDC settles directly to the wallet address you provide via the Coinbase facilitator. We see metadata only — receipts, hashes, idempotency keys.

What if my agent volume grows 10×? expand_more

Your Wiseble fee stays the same. You keep all the upside. That's the point of flat SaaS — your incentives stay aligned with your customers, not with us.

Will you introduce a take-rate at v2? expand_more

We may offer a take-rate option in the future as an alternative to SaaS — never as an additional fee. And only after legal review and a full opt-in mechanism.

Is there a free pilot? expand_more

Yes. Design partners run a free pilot in exchange for a public case study and feedback. Apply via the partner form.

What happens if I want to leave Wiseble? expand_more

Your customers, code, and wallet stay yours. Removing the middleware is a one-line revert. We don't lock you in technically, contractually, or commercially.
