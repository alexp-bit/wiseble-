// About

We make existing infrastructure agent-ready.

// Mission

The next decade of internet commerce will be conducted by autonomous AI agents.

But the infrastructure they need — APIs, data, tools — was built for humans. Sign-up forms. Card-on-file. Click-through ToS.

Wiseble bridges this gap, starting with web data.

We don't build new infrastructure. We make the existing infrastructure agent-payable.

// Principles

Five things we will not do.

  1. 01

    We never custody customer funds.

    Money flows agent → provider, on-chain, directly. We see metadata, never balances.

  2. 02

    We never replace our partners' billing.

    Stripe stays Stripe. Chargebee stays Chargebee. Your homegrown invoicing stays yours.

  3. 03

    We never put our brand in front of theirs.

    Agents see your service. Your name on the receipt. Your support inbox. Wiseble defaults to invisible.

  4. 04

    We charge predictable SaaS fees.

    Flat monthly, never percentage of volume. Your incentives stay aligned with your customers, not with us.

  5. 05

    We default to invisible.

    If we are doing our job, your customers — agents and humans — never need to know we exist.
